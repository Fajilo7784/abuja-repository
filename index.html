Abuja: City of Peace and Power

Overview

Abuja, the capital city of Nigeria, was purpose-built in the 1980s and symbolizes unity and national vision. Strategically located in the center of Nigeria, it was chosen to represent neutrality and accessibility for all regions of the country.
